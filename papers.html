Call for Papers

Original unpublished contributions are solicited for presentation at the 1st International Conference on International Conference on Intelligent Systems, Signal Processing, and Smart Grid Technologies - IEEE INSIGHT 2025. Papers are not allowed to be submitted in parallel to any other conference or journal.
Original papers are invited on the subject areas including, but not limited to, the following:

Track #01 - Intelligent Systems

  • Machine Learning and AI for Embedded Systems
  • Advanced Signal Processing Techniques in Intelligent Systems
  • IoT-Enabled Intelligent Systems
  • Smart Sensors and Actuators
  • VLSI Design for Intelligent Systems
  • Neural Networks and Fuzzy Logic in Control Systems
  • Cognitive Radio and Intelligent Communication Networks
  • Energy-Efficient Intelligent Systems
  • Real-Time Decision-Making Systems
  • Security and Privacy in Intelligent Systems

Track #02 - Signal Processing

  • Deep Learning for Signal Processing
  • Sparse Signal Processing and Compressed Sensing
  • Adaptive and Nonlinear Signal Processing
  • Multidimensional and Multimodal Signal Processing
  • Signal Processing for Wireless Communications
  • Quantum Signal Processing
  • Biomedical Signal Processing
  • Signal Processing in Smart Grids
  • Real-Time Signal Processing Systems
  • Signal Processing for Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Audio and Speech Signal Processing
  • Image and Video Signal Processing
  • Statistical Signal Processing
  • Cognitive Signal Processing
  • Signal Processing for Autonomous Systems

Track #03 - Power Electronics and EVs

  • Advanced Power Converter Topologies
  • Digital Control and Smart Power Electronics
  • Electric Motor Drives and Control
  • Charging Infrastructure and Emerging Battery Technologies
  • Integration of EVs with Grids
  • Vehicle Dynamics and Control

Track #04 - Smart Grids and Renewable Energy

  • Grid Architecture and Integration
  • Advanced Metering and Communication System
  • Energy Management and Efficiency
  • Power Quality and Reliability
  • Advanced Renewable Technologies
  • Economic policy and Global Perspectives
