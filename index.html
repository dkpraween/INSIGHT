About Vardhaman

Established in 1999, Vardhaman College of Engineering is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, Accredited by National Board of Accreditation for TIER-I, and All Indian rank 151-200 Band in NIRF.
Undergraduate Programme: B.Tech in multiple branches (11):

  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Computer Science and Machine Learning
  • CSE (AIML)
  • CSE (AIDS)
  • Computer Science and Data Science

Postgraduate Programme: M. Tech in multiple branches (6) and Master of Business Administration (MBA)

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Digital Electronics and Communication Systems
  • Power Electronics and Electric Drives
  • Embedded Systems
  • Engineering Design
  • Structural Engineering
